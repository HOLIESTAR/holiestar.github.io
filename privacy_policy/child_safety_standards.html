Child Safety Standards

Our app is designed for users aged 13 and above and focuses solely on managing and displaying notification content from other apps. It does not include messaging, social interaction, or user-generated content features. To ensure a safe experience for all users, including minors, we implement the following measures:

  • Age requirement: Users must be 13 years or older to use this app.
  • No communication features: This app does not support chat, messaging, or user interaction. It only reads and displays existing notifications from other apps with the userâ€™s consent.
  • No AI or content moderation needed: Since the app does not allow content creation or communication, content moderation is not applicable.
  • Privacy and security: We handle notification data locally and do not share it externally. All data is processed on-device, and no personal data is collected or stored.
  • Parental guidance recommended: We recommend that parents supervise younger users to ensure responsible use of notification access features.
