Google Drive Integration for Backup

To provide chat history backup and restore services, this APP offers integration with Google Drive. We interact with your Google Drive data in the following specific ways:

  • Specific Files (drive.file): We only access files that are created or opened by this APP. This is used specifically to save your encrypted chat history and media to your own Google Drive. We do not have permission to access, read, or modify any of your other private files or folders.
  • Application Data Folder (drive.appdata): We use this restricted-access folder to store essential configuration files related to your backup status. These files are hidden from your main Google Drive view.

Your backup data is stored directly in your personal Google Drive account. We do not transmit or store your Google Drive data on our own servers. Message Peeping Tom's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use your Google Drive data for advertising purposes, nor do we sell or share this data with any third parties.
